Cycle deadlines

The dates a cycle's schedule is computed from. The two division deadlines are found in the department calendar automatically — you only need to step in if the match is wrong or missing. Everything else — drafts due, deliberation, the vote, the letter, and the candidate review windows — is worked out backward from them (and, for promotions, forward from the letters-due date). We store the calendar's entry, not a copy of the date, so if a date moves upstream the schedule follows.

The Division sets two submission deadlines, split by how heavy the review is. Merit and salary-increase files go by the first; promotion and mid-career files — the ones with external letters — go by the second, which is earlier.
