Student notifications

The message emailed to a student when their petition is returned for revision or completed. Tokens {FirstName} and {Course} are filled in automatically. The essentials are always appended below the message and cannot be edited away: the class number and enrollment instructions, your note to the student, and the PDF link (completion); the sponsor's return note and the resubmission link (returned).

Import class schedule

Upload the registrar's "Schedule of Classes — Independent Studies" export (CSV) for a term. Instructors are matched to profiles; unmatched rows can be bound here, which adds the spelling to that person's alternate names for next time. Committing replaces the whole term's table.

Export term

Download a term's imported sections as a CSV — the registrar's columns plus the resolved instructor name, email, and how the match was made. The file is generated in your browser and opens directly in Excel or Sheets; nothing is written to Drive.
